Frequently Asked Questions

üöÄ Getting Started

What is EduConnect? ‚ñº
EduConnect is an online platform that connects students with qualified tutors across various subjects. We provide a simple, accessible way for students to find expert help and for educators to share their knowledge with eager learners.
How do I create an account? ‚ñº
Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, choose whether you're a student or tutor, fill in your details (name, email, password), and you're all set! Your account will be created instantly and you can start using the platform right away.
Is EduConnect free to use? ‚ñº
Yes! EduConnect is completely free for both students and tutors. Students can browse tutors, enroll in sessions, and access resources at no cost. Tutors can create sessions and connect with students without any fees.
What subjects are available? ‚ñº
We offer tutoring in over 30 subjects including Mathematics, Science, Programming, English, History, Languages, Arts, and many more. Our tutor community is constantly growing, adding new subjects and specializations regularly.

üë®‚Äçüéì For Students

How do I find a tutor? ‚ñº
After logging in, go to the "Browse Tutors" section. You can search by name, filter by subject, or browse all available tutors. Each tutor profile shows their subjects, rating, description, and available sessions.
How do I enroll in a tutoring session? ‚ñº
Find a tutor you like, click "View Sessions" to see their available tutoring sessions, then click "Enroll Now" on any session that fits your schedule. You'll be automatically enrolled and the tutor will be notified. You can view all your enrolled sessions in "My Sessions".
Can I message my tutor? ‚ñº
Yes! Once you're enrolled in a session, you can send messages to your tutor through the Messages section. Click on "Messages" in your dashboard, select the tutor, and start chatting. This is perfect for asking questions or clarifying details about the session.
How do I access educational resources? ‚ñº
Go to the "Resources" section in your dashboard. Here you'll find free educational materials shared by tutors and other students. You can filter by subject, search for specific topics, view resources, and download materials that help with your studies.
Can I share my own study materials? ‚ñº
Absolutely! Students can upload resources to help other learners. Click on "Upload Resource" in your dashboard, fill in the details about your material (title, description, subject), and upload it. Your contribution helps build our learning community!

üë®‚Äçüè´ For Tutors

How do I become a tutor on EduConnect? ‚ñº
Simply sign up and select "Tutor" as your account type. After registration, complete your profile by adding your subjects, a description of your expertise, and any relevant information students should know. Then you can start creating tutoring sessions immediately!
How do I create a tutoring session? ‚ñº
In your tutor dashboard, click "Create Session". Fill in the session details including title, subject, description, date, time, duration, and maximum number of students. Once created, students can immediately enroll in your session. You'll receive notifications when students join.
How do I manage my students? ‚ñº
Go to "My Sessions" to see all enrolled students for each session. You can also visit "My Students" to see all students who have ever enrolled in your sessions. From there, you can view student information and send them messages if needed.
Can I edit or delete a session? ‚ñº
Yes! Go to "My Sessions" and you'll see Edit and Delete buttons for each session. When editing, you can update the title, description, date, and time. If you delete a session, all enrolled students will be automatically notified about the cancellation.
How do I upload educational materials? ‚ñº
Click "Upload Resource" in your dashboard. Add the resource title, description, select the subject and file type, and specify the file name. Your resources will be publicly available to all students on the platform, helping grow the learning community.
How do notifications work? ‚ñº
You'll receive notifications when students enroll in your sessions, send you messages, or when there are important updates. Check the notification bell icon in the top right corner of your dashboard. You can mark individual notifications as read or mark all as read at once.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it? ‚ñº
On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive instructions to reset your password. If you don't receive an email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact our support team.
Is my data secure on EduConnect? ‚ñº
Yes. We take data security seriously and use industry-standard practices to protect your information. Your data is stored securely, and we never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
The platform isn't working properly. What should I do? ‚ñº
First, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. Make sure you're using an updated browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge). If problems persist, contact our support team through the Contact page with details about the issue you're experiencing.
Can I use EduConnect on mobile devices? ‚ñº
Yes! EduConnect is fully responsive and works great on smartphones and tablets. Simply visit our website from your mobile browser. We're also working on dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android that will be available soon.
