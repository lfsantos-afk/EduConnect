Quick Answers

Check out our FAQ for instant answers to common questions

How do I become a tutor?

Simply sign up with a tutor account, complete your profile, and start creating sessions. It's that easy!

Is EduConnect free?

Yes! Students can browse tutors and enroll in sessions for free. Tutors can also create and manage sessions at no cost.

How do I enroll in a session?

Browse available tutors, view their sessions, and click "Enroll Now" to instantly join any available session.
